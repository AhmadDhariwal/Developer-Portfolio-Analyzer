🎯 What this simulator does

It predicts how your Hiring Score and Job Match % would change if you added specific skills and built projects — before you invest the time. Results are role-aware: the same skill has different value for different roles.

📊 How scores are calculated

  • Skill Impact = relevance to role × difficulty × market demand × experience multiplier
  • Project Impact = complexity × duration × impact score × experience multiplier
  • Synergy Bonus = extra points when your skills match your project names
  • Time Penalty = applied when your plan exceeds realistic capacity for the duration
  • Max improvement is capped at +40 points to stay realistic

📝 How to fill the fields

  • Target Role — the job you are targeting. Skills are scored against this role.
  • Experience Level — juniors gain more from adding skills; seniors need deeper expertise.
  • Baseline Scores — your current scores from the Dashboard or Analysis page.
  • Duration — how many weeks you plan to work on this. Overloaded plans get penalized.
  • Skills — skills you plan to learn. Role-relevant skills score higher.
  • Projects — projects you plan to build. Higher complexity + longer duration = more impact.
  • Impact Score — how impactful the project is (0–100). A polished, deployed app = 80–90.

⚠️ Relevance tiers explained

  • Core — essential for this role (e.g. React for Frontend)
  • Valuable — useful but not primary (e.g. GraphQL for Frontend)
  • Transferable — general skill, some value in any role
  • Low Relevance — not typical for this role (e.g. React for Backend). Will still add a small score but with a warning.
