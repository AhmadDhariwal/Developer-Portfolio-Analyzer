Career Sandbox

Scenario Simulator

Model realistic what-if plans before you commit time. The deterministic engine estimates hiring score lift, job match change, confidence, and overload risk without touching your real profile.

What this sandbox does

It predicts how planned skills and projects can move your hiring score and job match, while keeping the math explainable and constrained by time, complexity, and role fit.

How the scoring works

  • Skills are weighted by role relevance, difficulty, demand, and your experience level.
  • Projects gain credit from complexity, duration, and delivery impact.
  • Aligned skills and projects earn a synergy bonus.
  • Overloaded plans are penalized so predictions stay realistic.

How prefills are used

Signals from GitHub, Resume Analyzer, Skill Gap, Recommendations, Career Sprint, Portfolio, and Integrations are used only for suggestions. They never overwrite your real scores or profile.

Good simulator habits

  • Keep scenarios focused to 2-4 important skills.
  • Pair one strong project with the skills you want employers to notice.
  • Use the confidence and uncertainty range before committing to an aggressive plan.
